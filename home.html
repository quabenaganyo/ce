The Establishment

Civilian Establishment is the civilian wing of the Ghana Armed Forces that provides mission essential support to the military by providing a workforce of talented, qualified civilians to fill critical non-combat roles.
The establishment's vision is "To project a highly formidable and well disciplined Civilian manpower capable and willing to support the Ghana Armed Forces."
